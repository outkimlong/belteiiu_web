“Welcome to Beautiful BELTEI Dormitory”

For the convenience of provincial and international students, BELTEI International University has constructed a luxurious 7-story dormitory building with 65 rooms of 130 beds capacity, a cafeteria, student lounge and syndicate rooms for student group discussion. Each bedroom contains a bathroom, kitchen and study table. We are ready to welcome national and international students for a comfortable stay with an affordable price. This dormitory will start to operate from January 1, 2022.
