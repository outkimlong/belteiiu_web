“English language is no longer your problem.”

English Intensive Course is designed to fill in the gaps and improve students’ ability to speak, to listen, to write and to read English, and for those who have enrolled in the National Program and have the intension to transfer to study in the International Program in Year 2 or to find a job after successfully completing their Year 1 study. The English Intensive Course is divided into two Courses equivalent to 30 credits, and which requires the students to study for one whole year. The students are allowed to choose a study shift that does not overlap with that of Year 1. Upon successfully fulfilling the course requirements, the university will issue a Certificate of Completion for the students.

❖ Terms and Conditions

Candidates of either sex, Cambodian or foreign students, monks, civil servants, company employees and the general public who are pursuing a Bachelor's or Associate Degree in Year 1in the National Program under any Faculty at BELTEI International University can register to study for free.
Candidates who are NOT pursuing a Bachelor's or Associate Degree in Year 1 in the National Program under any Faculty at BELTEI International University can also enroll by paying the tuition fee according to the set price list.
Students under the International Program or those of Year 2 and Year 3 under the National Program, and whose English proficiency is limited are also eligible to enroll by paying the tuition fee according to the set price list and with the approval of the President and His Excellency Dr., the Chairman.
