“Today a Teacher, Tomorrow a Leader.”

1. Course Overview

The Pedagogy Training Course is a modern study which helps improving cognitive strategies and teaching strategies of teacher-trainees in this digital era, as well as training in leadership with virtue and proper attitude. It is focused on pedagogical innovation as a direction for the development Human Capitals.

2. Requirement for Applicant

1. Students who have graduated or been studying Bachelor’s Degree and above are eligible to register for this Pedagogy Training.

2. Students may choose either program of the Pedagogy Training (GEd or ESL).

3. Students from other educational institutions who are studying in Year 1 and above are eligible to register for this Pedagogy Training and will be provided priority to work at BELTEI International schools.

3. Curriculum

The curriculum is designed to response 21st century pedagogy which aims to develop the skills and knowledge students need to succeed in working life. All subjects are prepared to offer teacher-trainees with implement innovation learning methods that integrated the use of technologies, inquiry, and problem-based approaches.

4. Leaning Outcomes

On successful completion of this program, students will be able to:

⚬ Administering continuous assessment of instructional process and learning outcomes

⚬ Select learning material that suits primary learners’ development

⚬ Make use of ICT to interact with other teacher and for professional development…

5. Career Opportunities

Upon the completion of training, students will become:

⚬ Leader

⚬ Professional teachers in primary level

⚬ Administrational principal

⚬ Standardized test developer

⚬ Educational Consultant

⚬ Curriculum designer

⚬ Educational or child psychologist

⚬ Textbook Author

⚬ Primary director

⚬ Tutor…
